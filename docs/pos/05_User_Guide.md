# POS User Guide

## Getting Started

1. Navigate to `/pos` in your browser
2. Open a shift by entering your opening cash amount
3. Start selling!

## Selling Products

### Search

- Type product name, SKU, or barcode in the search bar
- Results appear as you type (debounced 300ms)
- Click a result or use arrow keys + Enter to add to cart

### Barcode Scanning

- Place cursor in search field
- Scan barcode with physical scanner
- Product is automatically found and added to cart

### Quick Buttons

- Use the Quick Add section below search for frequently sold items
- Single-click adds product to cart immediately

## Cart Management

### Adjust Quantity

- Use +/- buttons on each cart item
- Or click the quantity to type a specific number

### Remove Item

- Click the trash icon on any cart item
- Or use the options menu (⋯) → Remove

### Apply Discount

- **Item discount:** Click discount icon on individual item
- **Cart discount:** Click % icon in the discount row of totals
- Choose percentage or fixed amount, select a reason

### Clear Cart

- Click "Clear" to remove all items (with confirmation)

## Payment

1. Click **Pay** button (or press F3)
2. Select payment method: Cash, Card, or Bank Transfer
3. Enter payment details:
   - **Cash:** Use numpad or quick amounts, change is calculated automatically
   - **Card:** Select card type, enter last 4 digits and approval code
   - **Bank:** Enter transfer reference
4. Toggle **Split Payment** to pay with multiple methods
5. Optionally attach a customer
6. Click **Complete Sale**

## Receipt

After completing a sale:

- **Print:** Click Print or press P
- **Email:** Click Email or press E, enter email address
- **New Sale:** Click New Sale or press N/Enter

## Hold & Retrieve

### Hold a Sale

1. Press F4 or click Hold
2. Enter optional reason
3. Cart is saved and cleared (max 10 holds)

### Retrieve a Held Sale

1. Press F5 or click Retrieve
2. Browse held sales list
3. Click the play icon to restore

## Shift Management

### Close Shift

1. Access shift close from the menu
2. Review shift summary (transactions, sales, expected cash)
3. Count physical cash by denomination
4. Review variance display
5. Add closing notes if needed
6. Click Close Shift

## Keyboard Shortcuts

| Shortcut  | Action                           |
| --------- | -------------------------------- |
| F2        | Focus product search             |
| F3        | Open payment modal               |
| F4        | Hold current sale                |
| F5        | Retrieve held sale               |
| Escape    | Close modal / Clear search       |
| P         | Print receipt (in receipt modal) |
| E         | Email receipt (in receipt modal) |
| N / Enter | New sale (in receipt modal)      |
| Delete    | Remove focused cart item         |
